The Problem
"Physical Keys Are a Liability."

Keys walk off lots. Paper logs get falsified. When a vehicle goes missing, damage is disputed, or an employee is involved in an incident - there is no reliable record. Just a paper log that may or may not be accurate. DOVA was built to solve that completely.

The DOVA Difference

Accountability Infrastructure.

Core Patent Asset
Three Gates. In Sequence. Every Time.

Identity verified via 2FA. Role confirmed for that vehicle. Manager approval received where required. No command reaches a vehicle until all three gates clear - in order. Machine-enforced, not policy-based.

Crown Jewel IP
Every Access. Permanently Documented.

Every access event is cryptographically bound to a real-time OBD-II diagnostic snapshot - odometer, fuel, battery state of charge, tire pressure, fault codes. Write-once. Tamper-evident. Legally admissible.

DOVA works on every U.S. vehicle since 1996 via the federally mandated OBD-II port. No OEM agreements. No infotainment access. One module, every make, every model.
