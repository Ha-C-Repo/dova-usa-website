The Problem

Physical Keys Are a Liability

Commercial fleets and dealerships lose millions each year to the same unsolved problem. Keys walk off lots. Paper sign-out logs get falsified. No one can prove who accessed which vehicle in what condition, or when. When a vehicle goes missing, when damage is disputed, when an employee is involved in an incident — there is no reliable record. Just a paper log that may or may not be accurate. DOVA was built to solve that problem completely.

The DOVA Difference

Not Just Access Control. Accountability Infrastructure.

Three-Gate Authorization

Three Gates. In Sequence. Every Time.

Identity verified via two-factor authentication. User role confirmed for that vehicle. Manager approval received where required. No command reaches a vehicle until all three gates clear — in order. Machine-enforced, not policy-based.

Diagnostic-Linked Access Log

Every Access. Permanently Documented.

Every access event is cryptographically bound to a real-time vehicle diagnostic snapshot — odometer, fuel level, battery charge, tire pressure, fault codes. Write-once. Tamper-evident. Legally admissible. The record exists before the dispute does.

Universal OBD-II Compatibility

Every Vehicle. No OEM Permission.

DOVA works on every U.S. vehicle manufactured since 1996 via the federally mandated OBD-II port. No OEM API agreements. No infotainment system access. No make/model-specific hardware. The same module on a Toyota, a Ford, a BMW, and a Chevy.
