The Problem

What DOVA Does

What DOVA Does for Automotive Retail.

  • Every test drive is identity-verified, gated by optional real-time manager approval, and logged with a full diagnostic snapshot before the vehicle is accessed.
  • Vehicle condition is captured at the moment of authorization: odometer, fuel, battery state of charge, tire pressure, and fault codes. One cryptographic snapshot per access event, written before the command reaches the vehicle.
  • Geofencing prevents unauthorized vehicle removal from your lot or service facility.
  • Managers approve access requests in real time from anywhere - no need to be present on the lot.
  • Access logs export to CSV or PDF for insurance claims, compliance reporting, or HR investigations.
  • No OEM API agreements required - compatible with every vehicle on your lot regardless of make or model.
What happens when the vehicle sells?
You have three options - redeploy the module to your next incoming vehicle, retain it through pre-delivery inspection, or transfer it to the buyer as a value-add subscription.
