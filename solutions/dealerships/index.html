DOVA for Auto Dealerships & Franchise Groups

Stop losing time to lost keys. Start building a record that protects your business.

The Problem

Keys Walk. Records Don't Exist. Disputes Have No Answer.

The average dealership team wastes 18 minutes per vehicle access event — searching for, signing out, and returning physical keys. Multiply that across your daily vehicle interactions and the cost is staggering. But the bigger problem isn't time. It's accountability. When a customer disputes damage at delivery, when a vehicle is moved overnight without authorization, when an employee is involved in an incident — what record do you have? A paper sign-out log that may or may not be accurate. DOVA replaces every one of those failure points with a cryptographic record.

What DOVA Does for Dealerships

  • Every test drive is identity-verified, manager-approved, and logged with a full diagnostic snapshot before the vehicle is accessed.
  • Vehicle condition is recorded at every access event — odometer, fuel, tire pressure, fault codes — before and after every interaction.
  • Geofencing prevents unauthorized vehicle removal from your lot or service facility.
  • Managers approve access requests in real time from anywhere — no need to be present on the lot.
  • Access logs export to CSV or PDF for insurance claims, compliance reporting, or HR investigations.
  • No OEM API agreements required — compatible with every vehicle on your lot regardless of make or model.
What Happens When the Vehicle Sells?
You have three options — redeploy the module to your next incoming vehicle, retain it through the pre-delivery inspection, or transfer it to the buyer as a value-add subscription.
