For Commercial Fleet Operators

DOVA for
Commercial Fleets.

Centralized access control, real-time visibility, and a permanent record - for every vehicle in your fleet.

The Problem
"You manage dozens of vehicles. You have almost no record of who drives them."

Physical key cabinets. Paper sign-out logs. A verbal policy of "ask your manager first." None of it is enforceable. None of it produces a record that survives a dispute, an insurance claim, or an HR investigation. When a vehicle is damaged, when mileage is unexplained, when a theft occurs - you are left with anecdotal evidence. DOVA was built specifically to solve this problem.

No enforceable record of who drove which vehicle
No proof of vehicle condition at handoff
No geofence to prevent unauthorized use
No diagnostic snapshot when an incident happens
What DOVA Does

What DOVA Does for Fleet Operators.

  • Role-based access control - each driver sees only the vehicles they're authorized to operate.
  • Manager approval gates - supervisors authorize access in real time, from anywhere.
  • Every access event logged with driver identity, GPS, and full vehicle diagnostic snapshot.
  • Geofencing restricts vehicle access to approved geographic zones.
  • Real-time GPS fleet map - see every vehicle, live, at all times.
  • Tamper detection - unauthorized module removal triggers an immediate GPS-stamped alert.
  • Access logs exportable for compliance, insurance, and HR purposes.
  • Universal OBD-II compatibility - works on every vehicle in your fleet regardless of make or model.
The Accountability Record

The Question Every Fleet
Manager Faces.

"Was the fuel tank full when Employee A took this vehicle on March 14th?" With DOVA, that question has a definitive answer. The diagnostic snapshot captured at every access event is permanently on record - odometer, fuel, battery state of charge, tire pressure, fault codes - cryptographically bound to the transaction, impossible to alter. For insurance disputes, HR investigations, and routine compliance audits, this record is invaluable.
