After the Sale

After the
Sale.

When a vehicle sells, DOVA gives you and your customer clear options. No loose ends. No surprises.

Download Post-Sale PDF

Your Business Options

Redeploy

Remove & Redeploy

Remove the module and reinstall on your next incoming vehicle. It auto-enrolls to the new VIN at power-on. Zero reconfiguration required. Full fleet ROI on every module.

Retain

Hold Through PDI

Keep the module installed through the pre-delivery inspection. Continue monitoring GPS location and vehicle diagnostics until keys are physically handed to the customer.

Sell With Car

Transfer to Buyer

Offer the DOVA module as a value-add with the sale. Transfer the vehicle to a customer account with a new subscription. A differentiator that turns a standard handoff into a premium experience.

Your Customer's Options

Clean Delivery

No Module

Module removed before delivery. Customer receives their vehicle with no DOVA hardware. Clean, simple, standard.

Personal Use

DOVA Personal Account

Customer activates a personal DOVA account - GPS tracking, remote lock/unlock, and vehicle health monitoring as a monthly subscription.

Business Use

DOVA Business Account

If the customer operates a fleet or business, they onboard the module directly into a DOVA business account with full fleet features active from day one.

Every option is managed from the DOVA web dashboard. No phone calls, no manual transfers, no paperwork.
